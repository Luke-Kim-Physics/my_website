The Church is the Body of the Lord

Publication Date: 2025-08-01

Preface

In my life, which was transformed upon meeting the Lord, the most glorious thing has been the opportunity God has granted me to serve His body, the Church. God is the true King who created this world; He is righteous and He is true life. We, as sinners, are beings who cannot dare to meet Him, nor do we possess anything to offer Him. And yet, to us who are so utterly lacking, God has bestowed the privilege of serving the Lord's body, allowing us to be used for the fulfillment of His will. In all our lives, could there possibly be a glory greater than this? It is through the Church that we have been enabled to hear His voice and to touch Him.

It is through the Church that we have been able to embrace Him, and that His love has come to overflow in my life and in yours. We must come to know that this is the spiritual reality.

Therefore, one who lives a life of faith ought to strive to love the Church in this day. If the Church is in pain, we must care for it as we would our own bodies. We must help the fullness of Him who fills all in all to flow out into the world through the Church.

Yet, many find it burdensome to watch over the Church, to aid the Church, and to hold a position of service. The reasons are as countless as the individuals, ranging from a reluctance to offer what is theirs to simply not knowing how to serve. Or perhaps, out of a humble heart, one might accept a position, only to wander in difficult moments, not knowing what to teach, or to teach what one does not truly believe, merely following the instructions of others.

Thus, I write this book to teach the universal framework of faith that must first be held by those who are appointed as office-bearers of the Church, before they endeavor to do anything in their roles. (Or rather, so that upon receiving an office, they may read this book multiple times and examine this clear framework within themselves time and again.) It is my earnest hope that every single person who reads this book will come to clearly know and believe this spiritual reality.

August 1, 2025
